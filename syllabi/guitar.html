Course Description:

Students will develop musical skills and refine guitar playing techniques in this course. Learning activities will include advanced note reading and playing scale patterns, a higher level of skill development in forming chords, picking, strumming, 5th position reading, rhythms, composing and playing songs. In addition to in-class activities, students will participate in performances at community events. If possible, students should provide their own acoustic guitar (preferred). A few school-owned guitars are available on a first come, first served basis.


Major Units of Study:

Units incorporate the National Common Core Arts Standards

Course Assignments:

  • Common Assessments (tests, daily playing competencies, performances, etc.) 60%
  • Formative Assessments (worksheets, misc. assignments, etc.) 10%
  • Creative Process (working collaboratively, practicing during class) 30%
