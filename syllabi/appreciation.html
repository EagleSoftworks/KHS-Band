RUNNING START COURSE NUMBER AND TITLE: HUMA 105W Introduction to Music


CATALOG DESCRIPTION FROM NHTI: This course offers a fundamental approach to the study of music history. Students will learn a foundational vocabulary describing the properties of music; explore various genres; acquire an improved facility to listening and appreciating music; learn to identify music from different periods; and broaden one’s understanding of a specialized area of interest through research.


GENERAL OBJECTIVES OF COURSE: Students will learn a foundational vocabulary describing the properties of music; explore various genres; acquire an improved facility to listening and appreciating music; learn to identify music from different periods; and broaden one’s understanding of a specialized area of interest through research.


COURSE OUTCOMES: Students will be able to

  • Understanding various areas of music and music education.
  • Comprehend various genres.
  • Identify music from different periods.

LEARNING ACTIVITIES: Lectures, discussions, listening, viewing, research
